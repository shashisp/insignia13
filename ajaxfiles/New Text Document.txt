<i><b>Household Electric Generator Using Bicycle</b></i>
</p>
<p style="font-size:14px; color:#FFF;">
<i><b>Design a system to generate electric energy using bicycle for household application.</b></i>
</p>
 <p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-size:18px; color:#F00;">
Rules and regulations:
</p>
 <p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="font-size:14px; color:#FFF;  margin-left:20px;">
<li>It’s solo / team event with team strength restricted to 4. </li>    
<li>Competition is open to students of for all branches of engineering studying in any semester.</li>
<li>Last date for sending proposal: 11-03-2013.</li>
<li>The selected teams have to present their proposal to the evaluating panel during INSIGNIA-13 on 21-3-13 at 11am. Each team will be given 15-30 minutes time for this.</li>
<li>No spot entry.</li>
<li>Registration fees: Rs 200/- per team and  is to be paid to “-- ---“.</li> 
<li>Students are requested to feel free to consult the coordinators for any other information and clarification.</li>
<li>The design proposal is to be submitted in both hard and soft form. The hard copy is to be sent to: Prof. Mrityunjaya Kappali, E&E Dept., S.D.M.C.E.T., Dharwad – 580 002, Karnataka.<br />
Sub: Engg. Design Contest – 2013. (sub must be mentioned)</li>
<li>The soft copy is to be sent to: edc.eee.insignia13@gmail.com</li>

</ul>
<p style="font-size:18px; color:#F00;">
Procedure:
</p>
 <p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul style="font-size:14px; color:#FFF;  margin-left:20px;">
<li>Net power capacity is to be suitably assumed  considering normal bicycle employed in India. Storage provision has to give one day backup.</li>  <li>Proposed system is to be an "Add On" to the existing bicycle with a provision to attach/ detach.</li>
<li>Working of the proposed arrangement is to be explained highlighting  briefly the role each component. </li>
<li>The system is to be designed bringing out the specifications of different components with suitable technical justifications. Cost estimation is to be provided.</li>
<li>Additional weightage will be given for implementation & demonstration of the  design. The design proposal can as well include display provisions for speed, voltage, power etc.</li>


</ul>
